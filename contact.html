Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit my own poems or stories?

Yes! We welcome guest contributions. Use the contact form above with subject "Collaboration / Guest Writing" and share your work.

क्या मैं अपनी कविताएँ यहाँ प्रकाशित कर सकता हूँ?

जी हाँ! हम अतिथि योगदान का स्वागत करते हैं। ऊपर दिए गए फॉर्म में "Collaboration" चुनें और अपनी रचना भेजें।

Is this website free to use?

Absolutely. KavyaLok is completely free — no registration required to read, bookmark, or share content.

How do I use the bookmark feature?

Click the 🔖 bookmark icon on any poem, story or shayari card. Bookmarks are saved locally in your browser — no account needed.
