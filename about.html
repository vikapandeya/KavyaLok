राजेंद्र प्रसाद शर्मा
✍️ Written by VIKH • Khushi & Vikash

Born in the cultural heartland of India, Rajendra Prasad Sharma discovered his love for words at a young age. Growing up surrounded by the rich oral tradition of Hindi poetry and the timeless shayari of Urdu literature, he developed a unique voice that bridges the classical and the contemporary.

📖 Literary Journey

Sharma's literary journey began with a single poem published in a local Hindi magazine in 1998. Within a decade, his debut collection "मन की गहराइयाँ" (Depths of the Mind) became a bestseller. His writing transcends language boundaries — equally celebrated in Hindi and English, his works have been translated into six Indian languages.

✦ My Philosophy of Writing

कविता लिखना मेरे लिए श्वास लेने जैसा है — बिना इसके जीना संभव नहीं। हर शब्द में एक आत्मा होती है, हर पंक्ति में एक सच होता है। मैं लिखता हूँ क्योंकि कुछ बातें सिर्फ कविता में ही कही जा सकती हैं।

— राजेंद्र प्रसाद शर्मा
